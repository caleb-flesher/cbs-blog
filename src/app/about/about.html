About the Builder

Favorite Trail: Dreamweaver - Snowshoe, WV Favorite Park: Snowshoe, WV Favorite Bike Brand: Norco

My name is Caleb - I am a born and raised Marylander and for the past ten years, I have journeyed in discovering my passion for bikes.

Now, I am building them!

I've created this blog page to share my love for bikes with the world. One day, I hope for this website to become the "hub" of where my bike builds will reside, either as an archive or actively for sale!

I'm also open to taking requests. As this is my pasttime hobby, I only seek to charge enough to maintain the shop, not necessarily grow it (not that I'm opposed to growth, however!).

Thanks for dropping in!

# We've bought an RV!

## May 3rd, 2026

A few weeks ago, we made a decision to buy a (used) RV that we had found on Facebook Marketplace. It's a 2006 Fleetwood Williamsburg, which is a popup camper. First impressions were exciting in that it was in fair condition, had all the amentities I was hoping for and plenty of room for us to enjoy a longer weekend or perhaps week+.

I know this doesn't really fit in with the them of this site, but hear me out - bike trips with the camper! Now having a place to call "home base" with a shower and AC, I feel like it gives us a lot of opprotunity to travel to new places with new trails and explore! One trip we have even planned is for a Bikes and Beers stop out in Delaware, hosted by my favorite brewey Crooked Hammock. I am stoked to stay there and ride the route to come back for brews and good times without having to pay extravagent costs for a hotel/AirBnB! Instead, we will have our own space to come back to post party.

__By the way, none other that the Notorious [Barhopper](https://calebsbikeshop.com/bike-builds/the-barhopper) will be riding at that bikes and beers event as well!__

We have been hard at work prepping the camper for it's first adventure and while it seems like it is still a long ways off the work is nearing completion. However we have hit our first official snag...
